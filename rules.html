COMMUNITY RULES

In-Game Rules

1. Random Death Match RDM

The act of killing someone for no reason is prohibited. You must have a reason to kill someone.

2. Vehicle Death Match VDM

The act of hitting someone with your car is prohibited. You must have a valid reason to hit someone.

3. New Life Rule NLR

Once you die, you are not allowed to go back to the prior scene, and kill the player that killed you.

4. Safe Zones Zones

Civ spawns, LEA areas, DOT station, Fire station, and the Gun store are protected zones.

5. Realistic Avatars RP

No unrealistic or animal avatars. If you wouldn't see it in real life, don't use it here.

6. Fail Roleplay FRP

Don't do things that aren't possible. If a gun is to your head, roleplay fear and compliance.

7. GTA Driving Driving

No pointless speeding or drifting. Realistic driving only, unless in an active police chase.

8. Impersonation Staff

Do not impersonate staff or whitelisted department members. This results in immediate moderation.

Discord Rules

Rule 1 - Discrimination

No discrimination against anyone for their race, background, or beliefs. Zero tolerance.

Rule 2 - NSFW Content

Strictly no NSFW related content in any of our channels. Keep it professional.

Rule 3 - Spamming

Spamming is not tolerated. Refrain from repeating the same phrase multiple times.

Rule 4 - Politics

Do not mention real-world politics at any moment while on this server.

Rule 5 - Phishing Links

No suspicious or unfamiliar links. Sending phishing links leads to a permanent ban.

Rule 7 - Alt Accounts

Alternative accounts are not tolerated. Caught users will be punished severely.

Rule 10 - Drama

Arguments or hostile behavior will not be tolerated. Communicate respectfully at all times.

Rule 13 - English Only

Use English in all public channels to ensure everyone understands the conversation.
