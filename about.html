OUR STORY.

The Vision

Alaska State Roleplay is a new immersive roleplay server based on the game Emergency Response: Liberty County. We didn't just want to make another server; we wanted to create a destination for those who value realism and storytelling.

From the rugged terrain of the frontier to the organized chaos of the urban centers, every detail is designed to make you feel like you are truly part of the Alaskan landscape. We hope to see you soon!

Core Foundations

Professionalism

Our staff and department heads are trained to maintain a high standard of conduct at all times.

Creative Liberty

We provide the tools; you provide the story. Your character's journey is entirely in your hands.

Supportive

Whether you are a veteran or new to ER:LC, our community is here to help you grow.
